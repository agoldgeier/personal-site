Music

I have been a pianist for over 18 years and a composer and songwriter for almost as long.

As a classical pianist, my playing has been featured on stage at the Los Angeles County Museum of Art’s Bing Theater, Prague’s Palffy Palace, on the radio on KCRW, LA’s NPR affiliate, and I actively work in New York as an accompanist for vocal lessons, rehearsals, and recitals.

I was the touring keyboardist for Daniel Bambaata Marley from 2011 to 2012, and have since played all corners of America with various other acts. I currently perform and record in New York primarily with With Snack and with my solo outfit, and have worked with other artists around the city, such as Mustardmind,Levi Robin, Dan Hemerlein, The Pine Hollows, Ben Yee-Paulson, and more.
